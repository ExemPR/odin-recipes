Lasagna Recipe

Recipe Title

Description

This lasagna recipe is a delightful combination of layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and plenty of cheese. It's baked to perfection, creating a mouthwatering dish that's perfect for gatherings or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and tomato paste to the beef mixture. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, layer the meat sauce, pasta, and cheese. Repeat layers.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  6. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving.
